About us

Bungani Technologies (PTY) LTD is a 100% Black owned Company Established in 2018. The company is solely owned by Sibongiseni Majova. Bungani Technologies is a fast track trading company which endeavours to provide products of exceptional quality and deliver services timeously. The company is individualized to ensure great sustainabily in the ever changing digital world

What we do:

focus on supplying all types of businesses from small to large with a variety of goods and services. We supply products that are of the best quality available from all leading brands. We offer delivery services throughout the country and we strive to use the most specialized sectors of professional services and being committed in our services to ensure efficiency, timeliness, quality and exceeding of clients’ expectations and needs .
